About me

I’m Kayla, a Senior Product Designer with a love for digital media and game design. I grew up playing games before my time, like Metroid on the Atari. When I was thirteen, I saved enough money to buy my own PS3, and from there my obsession with game art, digital media, and storytelling really took off.

By day, I work at Microsoft designing user-friendly apps and AI tools, building sleek interfaces, and creating storyboards to connect with customers.

I’m focused on merging two worlds — using my UI/UX skills while keeping my creative edge sharp with game art, illustrating, and storytelling.
